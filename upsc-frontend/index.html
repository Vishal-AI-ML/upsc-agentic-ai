FAQ

Everything you need to know

Common questions about UPSC AI and how it helps your preparation.

Is UPSC AI free to use?+
Yes. Sign up and start for free — no credit card needed. You get notes, current affairs, PYQ practice and evaluation right away.
Does the AI make up facts?+
It’s built specifically to avoid that. Notes, current affairs and questions are grounded on real sources (your PDFs, NCERT, and news feeds like PIB, The Hindu and Indian Express), and approximate or uncertain figures are clearly marked “(verify)”. Every AI-generated section also carries a short disclaimer reminding you to cross-check key facts — it won’t present guesses as certainty.
What can I study here?+
NCERT chapters, your own uploaded books/notes, daily and monthly current affairs, previous-year-style question practice, mains answer evaluation, and a personalised study planner with live exam dates.
Where do the current affairs come from?+
Daily current affairs are grounded in real headlines from trusted feeds — PIB, The Hindu, Indian Express and Down To Earth. If reliable news isn’t found, we tell you honestly instead of fabricating a digest.
Can I upload my own notes or books?+
Yes. Upload a text-based PDF and the AI generates grounded notes and lets you chat with the document. You can also build a personal question bank from your own PDFs.
Is my data private?+
Each account has its own secure login, and your history and uploads stay tied to your account. Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text.
